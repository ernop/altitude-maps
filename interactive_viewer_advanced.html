Keyboard Controls

W Move forward
S Move backward

D Strafe right
Q Move down (descend)
E Move up (ascend)

V Overhead camera view (reframe view)
R Reset camera
+ or = Increase sharpness (smaller buckets)
- Decrease sharpness (larger buckets)
